Meet the maker

My love affair with clay

It began in the early 1990s at a little stoneware studio. For years I kept pottery as a hobby while I worked in marketing — but it was a passion I wanted at the centre of my life.

I moved to Vancouver in 1998 and have made it my home ever since. Every piece I sell starts as a lump of clay on my wheel.

— Matthew
