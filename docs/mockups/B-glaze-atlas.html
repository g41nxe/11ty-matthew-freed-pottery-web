Designer's note — palette directions (click to compare)
A · Clay & Cobalt Built — recommended Terracotta, mocha & sand from the Tofino and Pemberton Earth glazes — the clay body itself. Warm earth against cobalt is the color story of his actual work, and it keeps the old site's near-white ground.
B · Charcoal Speckle Semi-matte charcoal & speckled grey — his most recurring glaze family (Yaletown, Strathcona, Zen). Moody and gallery-like, but risks reading somber next to warm product photography.
C · Kitsilano Air The lilac and grey-blue of Kitsilano & Jericho. Quiet and contemporary, but cool-on-cool gives less lift against navy and needs care for text contrast.

Twelve years at the wheel.
